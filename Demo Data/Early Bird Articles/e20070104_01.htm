Correction

A Jan. 3 article incorrectly said that U.S. Navy vessels patrolling the East African coastline near Somalia had been dispatched from facilities in Djibouti. The ships are part of the U.S. Navy's Fifth Fleet, based in Bahrain.

Editor's Note: The article by Karen DeYoung appeared in yesterday's Current News Early Bird.
